Hey students! Welcome to our Griffith course support channels. These chat rooms are to help us band together, share knowledge, and support one another. Whether you’re stuck on an assignment, revising for exams, or just want to swap ideas, you’ll find a space for your course here. Let’s learn, grow, and succeed as a community.
